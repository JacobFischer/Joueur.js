Class: Unit

Stardash. Unit

A unit in the game. May be a corvette, missleboat, martyr, transport, miner.

new Stardash.Unit()

Initializes a Unit with basic logic as provided by the Creer code generator. Never use this directly. It is for internal Joueur use.
